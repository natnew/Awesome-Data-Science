# Governance & Ethical Charter

## 1. Project Mission
Our mission is to democratize Data Science education by providing high-quality, free, and accessible resources for learners at all levels.

## 2. Ethical Charter
We are committed to promoting responsible Data Science.
- **No Malicious Use:** We will not accept resources that promote the generation of malware, misinformation, or non-consensual content.
- **Privacy First:** Resources must respect user privacy and data protection laws.
- **Bias Awareness:** We prioritize resources that address or mitigate algorithmic bias.

## 3. Scope
We accept:
- Open-source tools and libraries.
- High-quality educational content (free or paid, if clearly marked).
- Datasets that are legally shareable.

We do NOT accept:
- SEO spam or marketing-heavy content with low educational value.
- "Warez" or pirated material.
