## Expected Limitations

- Statistical power calculations require domain-specific effect size estimates
- Cannot replace institutional review board (IRB) approval for human subjects research
- Assumes independence of observations unless explicitly stated otherwise
- May not account for all confounding variables without complete context

## Ideal Context of Use

### Best Suited For:
- Designing A/B tests for product features
- Sample size calculations for statistical power
- Randomization strategies and treatment assignment
- Hypothesis formulation and test selection
- Educational purposes in experimental statistics

### Not Recommended For:
- Medical or clinical trials without regulatory expertise
- Experiments with ethical considerations requiring IRB review
- Complex multi-armed bandit scenarios without specialized algorithms
- Observational studies requiring advanced causal inference methods
